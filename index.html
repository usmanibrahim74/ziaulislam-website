About Our Foundation

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2015, Zia ul Islam Foundation was born from a deep commitment to serve humanity with compassion and dignity. We believe that every individual, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to basic necessities, education, and healthcare.

Our foundation focuses on supporting orphaned children, widowed women, and economically disadvantaged families across rural and urban communities. Through sustainable programs and transparent operations, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and create pathways to self-sufficiency.

With the unwavering support of our donors and volunteers, we've touched over 15,000 lives and raised more than ¬£2.5 million to fund critical initiatives. Every penny donated is accounted for and directed toward maximum impact.
